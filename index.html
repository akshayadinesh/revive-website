ReVIVE is a Virtual Reality app and website that helps make ADHD diagnosis and treatment easier, faster, and cheaper for patients and doctors alike.

Devpost Page

About reVIVE

Currently, the old ADHD diagnosis process takes 6-9 months and thousands of dollars. It is also extremely subjective (depending on the physician) and is prone to misdiagnosis. Our app seeks to solve both of these problems by bringing quanitifiable, real-time data to the diagnostic process.

The initial prototype of reVIVE, built for HTC Vive, won the Grand Prize at TechCrunch Disrupt NY Hackathon 2017, and is currently still in development. We are working with top researchers and professionals to refine our product and do user testing, but reVIVE will be released soon!

What Makes Us Unique

Although research has been done in the many health applications of VR, a diagnostic tool like ours has not yet been released on the market.

Fully Immersive

Virtual Reality technology creates a realistic simulation for patients.

Website & SMS

Doctors and physicians can keep updated through our website and messaging service.

Data Driven

Users can view progress over time, with data from sentiment analysis and simulation scores.

Faster, More Efficient

While ADHD diagnosis usually takes months and thousands of dollars, reVIVE makes the process easier and faster!
